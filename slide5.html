How do researchers measure anxiety and depression?

Each survey respondent answered four questions related to their mental health, taken from well-established mental health questionnaires.

Anxiety Depression
Questionnaire PHQ-2 GAD-2
Example question “Over the last 7 days, how often have you been bothered by ... having little interest or pleasure in doing things?” Over the last 7 days, how often have you been bothered by the following problems ... Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge?
Scoring Several days = 1 point; more than half the days = 2 points; nearly every day = 3 points
A score of three points or higher in either category counts as a “yes.”		 Several days = 1 point; more than half the days = 2 points; nearly every day = 3 points
