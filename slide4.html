Overview of Our Primary Data Source

Household Pulse Survey by NCHS and Census Bureau

  • Purpose: Rapidly monitor mental health changes during the pandemic.

  • Start Date: April 23, 2020.

  • Method: 20-minute online survey.

  • Focus: Frequency of anxiety and depression symptoms over the reference period.

  • Initial reference period: Last 7 days.

  • Updated reference period (from July 21, 2021): Last 14 days.

  • Data Insights: Percentage of adults reporting symptoms linked to generalized anxiety disorder or major depressive disorder.

  • Relevance: Provides timely, relevant information on the mental health impact of the pandemic in the U.S.
